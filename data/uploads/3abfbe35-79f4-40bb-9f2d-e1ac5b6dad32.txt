DESCRIPTION OF CHANGE:
================================================================

Additional waterproofing scope and associated remediation work
at the balcony assemblies on floors 11 through 14, north and
south elevations. Work includes:

1. Removal of previously installed waterproofing membrane on
   affected balcony areas where substrate cure requirements
   were not verified at time of original installation.

2. Surface preparation and re-priming of concrete substrate
   in accordance with manufacturer's requirements, including
   moisture vapor emission testing prior to re-application.

3. Re-application of Tremco TREMproof 250GC waterproofing
   membrane per manufacturer's published installation
   requirements and the architect's response to RFI No. 47
   dated June 13, 2022.

4. Field quality assurance testing (electronic leak detection)
   of re-installed membrane prior to topping slab placement.

5. Coordination with Coastal Waterproofing Systems, Inc. for
   re-installation work. Coastal shall perform the removal
   and re-installation under its original subcontract scope
   at no additional cost to Meridian; the costs reflected in
   this Change Order represent additional work beyond the
   Coastal subcontract, including substrate remediation,
   testing, and extended general conditions.

================================================================
REASON FOR CHANGE:
================================================================

During the course of the balcony waterproofing sequence on
floors 11 through 14 in July and August 2022, it was determined
that certain areas of the membrane installation did not fully
conform to the manufacturer's published installation
requirements as clarified in the architect's RFI No. 47
response. Specifically, portions of the installation proceeded
prior to verification of substrate cure criteria.

To ensure long-term performance of the balcony waterproofing
system and to protect the Owner's interest in a durable
installation, Meridian recommends removal and re-installation
of the affected areas. This Change Order provides for the
additional scope and associated costs.

Note: The original installation issue was identified by
Meridian's superintendent on September 28, 2022, during a
routine quality walkthrough of the balcony assemblies.
Corrective action was initiated immediately upon discovery.
